How It Works

A clinical process.
A simple experience.

Pupsona translates complex genetic and health data into an actionable nutrition plan — no veterinary degree required. The science stays under the hood so you can focus on your dog.

1

Upload DNA or Build Profile

Upload PDF, CSV, or TXT files from Embark, Wisdom Panel, DNA My Dog, or Orivet — or manually fill out our 5-step intake form covering breed, health, and lifestyle.

2

AI Runs the Analysis

The model cross-references genetic markers, breed predispositions, and health data against AAFCO and NRC dietary guidelines to build a precise nutritional profile.

3

Receive Your Full Plan

Daily caloric targets, macronutrient splits, ingredient recommendations, supplement dosages, foods to avoid, and a 7-day meal schedule — all organized in a clean results dashboard.
